Our Programes

Creativity

We foster creative minds with big ideas, vision management and goal setting. The beauty of individuality is that we all look at things differently and we should be thinking differently. This can be bogged down by the status quo. At Kid Koderz, unleash your individual creative spirit, and combine it with technical skills to be a powerful force for change.

Coding

Kid Koderz wants you to hack anything that pushes society forward. Want to advance your Java, HTML, CSS, C++, Python or Ruby skills? we have plenty of coding language courses that will give you industry level credibility and fill your skills tool box with nifty new skills

Robotics

Invent a new friend from scratch using circuits, legos, VEXâ¢, FIrst Robotics and custom Kid Koderz blueprints. Create a transformer robot, helpful robot to get those chores done more efficiently, or even a pancake flipping robot! The possibilities are really endless. So is the help around the house

3D- Printing

Have your parents said no to that cool figurine at the local Toys R Us? or Parents are purchasing too many figurines taking a toll on your wallet? Teach your kids how to print their own creations in as little as 3 hours, and equip your child with modern 3D printing, design and blueprinting skills.

Design Thinking

Have a great idea? Want to learn how to ideate? Join a design thinking class. We’ll go through a sophisticated learning model, validated by leading higher- ed institutions to help guide your ideation process, or simply work with an instructor to bring your current ideas to life.

Entrepreneurship And Innovation

You have a great idea and now you’ve invented it and have a basic prototype. The next step is to monetize it by bringing it to market and selling. Kid Koderz qualified instructors will help your child get through this process in a fun, educational and progressive way.
